Online IoT Internship

Welcome to our Online IoT Internship Program!

About the Internship

Our internship program offers a comprehensive learning experience in the Internet of Things (IoT). Participants will gain practical skills in working with sensors, microcontrollers, and other relevant technologies.

Internship Details

  • Dates: 01/06/2024
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Location: Online
  • Requirements: Basic understanding of electronics and programming concepts

What You'll Learn

  • Introduction to IoT concepts and applications
  • Working with sensors and actuators
  • Microcontroller programming (e.g., Arduino, Raspberry Pi)
  • Communication protocols (MQTT, HTTP, etc.)
  • Data collection and analysis
  • Building and deploying IoT prototypes
  • Introduction to IoT platforms (AWS IoT, Azure IoT, etc.)
  • Security considerations in IoT
